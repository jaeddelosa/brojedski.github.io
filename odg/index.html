The challenge

Open data is scattered — and often hard to find.

Researchers, analysts, and data enthusiasts waste hours hunting for reliable datasets across dozens of portals, each with its own interface and access rules. Valuable government, scientific, and economic data remains siloed — or worse, undiscovered.

Open Data Gateway solves this by providing a single, curated directory of the world's most trusted open databases — from NASA exoplanet archives to UN climate indicators, WHO health data, World Bank economic metrics, and hundreds more. All verified, all openly accessible, all in one place.

Who benefits

For researchers, data scientists, and curious minds.

Whether you're an academic hunting for clean datasets, a journalist fact-checking global trends, a startup building data-driven products, or simply a lifelong learner — Open Data Gateway cuts through the noise.

No more scattered bookmarks. No more paywalls. Just direct access to the world's most valuable open knowledge bases, organized and ready for discovery.

⚙️ Built for discovery

Each database is manually vetted and categorized, with direct links to the source. The directory is updated continuously as new open data portals emerge — ensuring you always have the latest access to credible information.
