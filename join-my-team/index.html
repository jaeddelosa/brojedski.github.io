📋 Requirements (non-negotiable)

  • You've built real automation workflows — Not tutorials. Show me n8n, Make, Zapier, or custom code that actually ran in production.

  • You handle errors — Your work includes logging, retries, or fallbacks. No silent failures.
  • You respect data privacy — No hardcoded secrets, no logging PII. Basic security hygiene.
  • For agentic AI roles: Experience with LangGraph, CrewAI, AutoGen, or similar. You've built an agent with tools or memory.
  • You communicate clearly — English written fluency. You say "I need help" or "I'll be late" before the deadline, not after.
  • You can work at least 10 hours/week consistently — Freelance or part-time, but reliable.
