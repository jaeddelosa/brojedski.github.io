The challenge

Legal information exists — but it's scattered, complex, and locked away.

Ordinary Filipinos struggle to find reliable legal guidance. Many databases require expensive subscriptions. Resources are spread across dozens of government websites. And legal language is overwhelming without proper context.

LegalBridge PH solves this by providing a single, curated directory of completely free legal resources — from Philippine laws and Supreme Court decisions to international treaties and legal aid services. All carefully organized for maximum accessibility.

Quick legal reference

380+ frequently asked questions with detailed answers and legal bases.

From constitutional rights to everyday legal questions — employment issues, marriage laws, property ownership, debt, online privacy, traffic violations, and more. Perfect for law students, bar reviewees, and citizens seeking practical legal guidance.

Example: "What is the hierarchy of laws in the Philippine legal system?" — Constitution, treaties, statutes, presidential issuances, administrative rules, local ordinances. Lower laws must conform to higher ones; the Constitution is supreme.
