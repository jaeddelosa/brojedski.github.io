About

My professional journey began in the public sector, managing large-scale data collection projects where I first recognized the power of structured information. This foundation, paired with a Bachelor's in Sociology, provided me with a unique lens: the ability to decode cultural nuances and human behavior within complex systems.

Leveraging this perspective, I transitioned into the global freelance market, collaborating with international clients to scale their impact through content, marketing, and strategic automation. Today, I sit at the intersection of behavioral psychology and technical orchestration. I specialize in building high-conversion acquisition funnels that prioritize human-centric design, ensuring that automation drives growth while maintaining a steadfast commitment to data privacy and ethical engagement.
