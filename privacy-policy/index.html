Privacy Policy

Effective Date: March 24, 2026

1. Introduction

This portfolio website is maintained by Jaed Delosa to showcase professional experience, projects, and services. This Privacy Policy explains what information is collected, how it is used, and your rights as a visitor.

2. Information Collected

  • No personal data collection: The site does not collect, store, or process personal information such as names, emails, or phone numbers.
  • Automatic logs: GitHub Pages may collect anonymous technical information (browser type, device, approximate location) for performance and security purposes.
  • External links: The portfolio may link to third‑party sites (LinkedIn, resume documents, project demos). Visiting those sites is subject to their own privacy policies.

3. Use of Information

Any automatically collected technical data is used only for:

  • Ensuring the website loads correctly
  • Monitoring performance and security
  • Improving accessibility

No personal profiling, marketing, or resale of data occurs.

4. Cookies

This site itself does not use cookies. However, GitHub Pages or linked third‑party services may use cookies for analytics or login purposes. You can manage cookies through your browser settings.

5. Data Sharing

  • No personal data is shared, sold, or disclosed to third parties.
  • External services (e.g., LinkedIn, GitHub repositories) operate under their own policies.

6. Security

The site is hosted on GitHub Pages, which provides HTTPS encryption and standard security practices. No sensitive data is stored or transmitted through this portfolio.

7. Your Rights

As a visitor, you have the right to:

  • Access the site without providing personal data
  • Control cookies through your browser
  • Contact the site owner with questions or concerns

8. Changes to This Policy

This Privacy Policy may be updated periodically to reflect changes in site content or hosting practices. Updates will be posted on this page with a revised effective date.

For questions about this Privacy Policy, please reach out at info@jaeddelosa.tech.
