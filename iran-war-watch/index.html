The Oil Crisis Tracker

20% of global oil transits through the Strait of Hormuz.

Iran controls the eastern shore of the Strait of Hormuz — through which 20% of all global oil transits daily. Any closure, even partial, triggers immediate supply shocks across Asian, European, and US markets.

The dashboard's Oil & Energy filter surfaces every article touching crude prices, OPEC decisions, tanker movements, pipeline security, and the macroeconomic fallout of supply disruptions — all sourced live from OilPrice.com, Reuters, and the financial desk feeds of major newsrooms.
